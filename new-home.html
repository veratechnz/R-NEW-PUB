      A About REINZ

      The Real Estate Institute of New Zealand (REINZ) is a membership organisation representing more than 14,000 voluntary real estate professionals nationwide.

      REINZ specialises in all facets of real estate including residential sales, rural, auctioneers, business brokers, commercial and industrial sales and leasing and residential property managers.

      Members of the REINZ aspire to best practice standards and are bound by the REINZ Codes of Agency Practice and Individual Membership.

      The REINZ brand is a mark of quality having been in business for more than 100 years.

      Codes of Practice

      Although REINZ is not the industry regulator we understand it is essential for our members to adhere to the rules set out by the Real Estate Agents Authority (REAA).

      B REINZ Statistics

      REINZ are proud to be able to supply members, media and the general public with monthly statistics press releases. The statistics in these press releases come from our member companies who kindly supply their data to REINZ each month.
