      Skills

      The Skills Organisation is the industry training organisation for the real estate industry. It is responsible for developing and maintaining quality training systems for the sector, enabling real estate professionals to achieve qualifications through on-job training.

      Training is conducted on an online basis with the support of skilled and experienced mentors. Anyone employed or engaged by a licensed real estate company and who wishes to further their career can access this on-job training.

      Qualifications offered by The Skills Organisation include:

      • The National Certificate in Real Estate (Salesperson) (Level 4)
      • The National Certificate in Real Estate (Branch Manager) (Level 5)
      • The National Diploma in Real Estate (Agent) (Level 5)
      • The New Zealand Certificate in Residential Property Management (Letting Agent) (Level 3)
      • The New Zealand Certificate in Residential Property Management (Property Manager) (Level 4)

      All the qualifications are developed in consultation with the real estate industry, with periodic reviews to ensure their continued relevance.

      Visit The Skills Organisation or Ph: 0508 754 557.

      The Real Estate Agents Authority (REAA)

      The REAA is the licensing and disciplining body for the real estate industry. It is an independent government agency that sits under the Ministry of Justice and runs as a separate organisation to REINZ. The job of the REAA is to promote a high standard of service and professionalism in the real estate industry and help protect buyers and sellers of property. It provides information for buyers and sellers, guidance for agents and how to become an agent and deals with complaints about agents’ behaviour.

      Ph: 0800 FORREAA or 0800 367 7322.
