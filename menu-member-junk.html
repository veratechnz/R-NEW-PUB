National Smoke Alarm Services

National Smoke Alarm Services provides REINZ members with 20% off the FireAngel ST620 smoke alarms. This is an important service for REINZ members because:

  • New Regulations will require the installation of long-life photoelectric smoke alarms in all properties subject to a Residential Tenancies agreement
  • International evidence indicates that having operational smoke alarms can reduce fire fatalities and injury by up to one half and reduce damage to the landlord’s property 
  • The new regulations are designed to improve the health and safety of rented properties and prevent death and injury as a result of fire.
  • Landlords will have to comply with the new regulations by October 2016 with the possibility of penalties of up to $3,000 for non-compliance.

The FireAngel smoke alarm comes with a 10 year sealed for life, tamper proof battery and the groundbreaking Thermoptek inside.
