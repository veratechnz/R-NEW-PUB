National Smoke Alarm Services

National Smoke Alarm Services provides REINZ members with 20% off the FireAngel ST620 smoke alarms. This is an important service for REINZ members because:

  • New Regulations will require the installation of long-life photoelectric smoke alarms in all properties subject to a Residential Tenancies agreement
  • International evidence indicates that having operational smoke alarms can reduce fire fatalities and injury by up to one half and reduce damage to the landlord’s property 
  • The new regulations are designed to improve the health and safety of rented properties and prevent death and injury as a result of fire.
  • Landlords will have to comply with the new regulations by October 2016 with the possibility of penalties of up to $3,000 for non-compliance.

The FireAngel smoke alarm comes with a 10 year sealed for life, tamper proof battery and the groundbreaking Thermoptek inside.

Mobil Member Benefit

Welcome to the fuel card that helps you run things your way

Petrol – 10.35 cents per litre (including GST)

Diesel – 10.35 cents per litre (including GST)


  • Q: Who can get a Mobilcard?
  • A: Any REINZ member agency and their staff.

  • Q: What is the discount?
  • A: REINZ members are eligible for the following discount off the pump price for petrol and diesel:
  • Petrol – 10.35 cents per litre (including GST)
  • Diesel – 10.35 cents per litre (including GST)

  • Q: Does this discount apply to exiting card holders?
  • A: Yes it does, from 6 June 2014, all Mobilcard holders that are members of REINZ will receive the above discounts on petrol and diesel (remember, Mobil needs to know you are a REINZ member for these discounts to apply).

  • Q: How long will it take to process my application and to get my/our Mobilcard(s)?
  • A: The process normally takes two weeks upon receipt of the application form.

  • Q: Where can I use my Mobilcard?
  • A: You can use your Mobilcard at any Mobil branded service station or stations and outlets that has a Mobilcard accepter sticker. This includes all New World and Pak ‘n Save stores with where fuel is available on site. Visit our site locator for a full list or to find one near you.

  • Q: What costs are involved in setting up and using Mobilcard?
  • A: None. All transaction fees (15 cents plus GST per transaction) and card fees ($30 per card for 3 years) have been waived as part of the REINZ membership offer.

  • Q: Can you manage your account online?
  • A: Yes you can order new Mobilcards, change PIN numbers, and review transactions on the Mobilcard webpage www. mobilcard.co.nz. You will need a login ID from the Mobilcard Support Centre, just call 0800 732 277.

  • Q: What happens if I lose my card?
  • A: You can call our Mobilcard Support Centre 24/7 on 0800 732 277 OR go online towww.mobilcard.co.nz and stop your card. In order to use this online service, you will need to have arranged prior administration access.

  • Q: Where is your call centre?
  • A: Our Mobilcard call centre is located in Auckland.

  • Q: What purchases restrictions are available?
  • A: Mobilcard currently has over 70 different restrictions, most common are fuel and oil only, please call Mobilcard Sales on 0800 662 458 or by emailing mobilcardsales@exxonmobil.com for more details.

  • Q: What security features are available?
  • A: Mobilcard provides PIN’s on all cards, purchase restrictions, transaction limits, daily spend limits and monthly spend limits on each individual card.

  • Q: How do I apply?
  • A: Open and print this application form. Alternatively you can call Mobilcard sales on 0800 662 458 to request a Mobilcard application. 0800 662 458

You must advise our team that you are a REINZ member to retrieve the benefits.

Please note: Discount maybe subject to review.

REINZ receives 1 cent per litre rebate on member purchases which is invested into member products and services.
